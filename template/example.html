1. To begin your ezSearch type in one or more keywords that will be searched for in the contracts that you would like to find.
2. Click on the Go button. A list of contracts will be displayed that matches your search criteria in the Results page section. To remove your search criteria text, click the Clear button.
3. You can refine your search by using the four filter boxes on the left side of the screen. You can drill down by Department Full Name, Contracting Agency Name, Full Legal Business Name and Treasury Account Symbol. After selecting a value from one of the filter boxes, you can further drill down on other available filters. The information in each filter box is displayed in descending order. Only the top 10 results will be displayed. Click on the appropriate link in each filter box. Your results will be displayed in the Results Page section.
4. Clicking on the links inside the documents allows you to further refine your search and drill down to specific information. For example by clicking on a specific PSC Code you will only see contracts for that PSC Code.
